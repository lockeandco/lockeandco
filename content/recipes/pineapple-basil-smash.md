---
title: Pineapple Basil Smash
type: recipe
ingredients: |-
  * 2.5oz Locke + Co Rye
  * 6 pineapple chunks
  * 4 basil leaves
  * 1/4oz lemon juice
  * 1/2oz Agave
preparation: >-
  1. Muddle basil and pineapple chunks, add lemon juice and agave syrup and
  whiskey.  

  2. Shake for at least 30secs very very hard.  

  3. Strain over ice.
